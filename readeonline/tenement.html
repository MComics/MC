TENEMENT 01

opens with a peek into the lives of the seven people in this story. Justin deals drugs to take care of his mom. Tanya plays the guitar and has hit a block. With a sick wife and unpaid bills to take care of, Bob tries to put his money into races. Gary is fed up with the buzzing sound coming from Felix's apartment next door. Oblivious to the festering darkness, they go through their lives. But Isaac, an observant kid who lives with his mother, Amanda, can sense the eyes on him, preparing to swallow the people in the building whole.
